What is technology and how does it benefit us?

Here I am going to talk about technology and it’s advancements. Technology comes from two Greek words ’Techne ’ which means arts or craft and ’logos ’ means thought or principle. Combining them forms ’Tekhnologia’ this was changed to English in today’s language as ’technology ’.

The first form of technology was the wheel. This allowed for other things to be created like tools from materials like iron or bronze which led to ships being made to increase efficiency to the point in todays day and age where phones, internet and now A.I (artificial intelligence) is created. This doesn’t stop there as there are many things that can be done with A.I that may allow new technology to be discovered. Technology is everywhere and we are very dependent on it.

You don’t believe me? How are you viewing this website? I guarantee that if you look around, there is something technology-related.

Technology is not limited to wires and motherboards etc, it can be in other forms; like stone tools being one of the earliest pieces of technology to help create something for the benefit of humanity. Today it has come to a point where we make prosthetic limbs for amputees with the help of technology.

The overall history of technology is a long and fascinating one, and it continues to shape the way we live and interact with the world around us.
